West Chester Elevated Lacrosse Club

The Elevated lacrosse program is based around skill progression and player development.  Players improve at practice, and are tested in games.  We will have a practice to game ratio of approximately 4-1. This model will lead to individual and team success by building individual skills, fundamentals, lacrosse IQ and team concepts.  The priority is that every player has fun, gets better at lacrosse and has the opportunity to succeed at the next level.

Every team will have a coach with high school or college coaching experience.

Director: Dan Deckelbaum West Chester Boys’ Director of Lacrosse, Avon Grove offensive coordinator, district and state champion high school coach, Elevated Lacrosse president, former MLL and NLL player.
