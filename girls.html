WCLA  girl's lacrosse is intended to support participation from Elementary to Middle School age youth in the West Chester Area School Districts.Our mission is to promote the game of lacrosse and to provide a safe and fun opportunity while emphasizing the importance of good sportsmanship, fitness and teamwork.

WCLA Girls Lacrosse is for residents of the West Chester Area in grades KG through 8th. WCLA Girls Lacrosse is affiliated with the Philadelphia Area Girl’s Lacrosse Association (PAGLA), and is a non-profit organization run by volunteers.  NOTE: You must reside within the West Chester Area School District to play in WCLA.


WCLA Girls Lacrosse’s Goal is to teach players fundamental skills for success on and off the field. Foster sound fundamentals in a fun and upbeat learning environment. Teach individual skills with an understanding of the relationship to team concepts. Prepare each player for the next level of lacrosse.


All Players will need the following lacrosse equipment to play: 1 - Colored mouth guard 1 - Protective eye guard 1 - GIRLS Lacrosse Stick *Cleats and/or turf shoes are recommended, but not required.


We expect all players to be committed to their team mates, coaches, and to the program. We understand that periodically there will be conflicts, but your teammates and coaches expect you to attend practices and games. Practices for Grades KG, 1/2, 3/4, 5/6, and 7/8 will be held at Rustin HS, Kelly Field or other fields within the West Chester Area.


Practice/Games field locations, days, times, and team rosters will be posted on this website after Player Evaluations late February/early March.


5/6 & 7/8 Teams will be formed based on the PAGLA “A”& “B” system. West Chester will host Player Evaluations late February/early March. We will not accept requests to be placed on a specific team or with particular coach and/or friends. The coaching staff and West Chester Youth Directors will assign each player to a team.

EQUIPMENT

All Players will need the following lacrosse equipment to play:

  • Girls Lacrosse Stick
  • Girls Goggles
  • Colored Mouth Guard (No clear or white)
  • Grass Fields > Cleats are acceptable
  • Turf Fields > Sneakers, Turf Shoes, & Cleats are acceptable
  • Players are to bring their own Water/Sports Drink to Practices andGames
  • 3rd – 8th Grade Players are to wear team issued pinnies to all practices and games
  • KG/1/2 Grade Players are to wear team issued tshirts to all practices and games
