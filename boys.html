The following West Chester Lacrosse Association (WCLA) Spring 2017 Boys Program Summary contains important information on:

Division Assignments:

Players are NOT permitted to “play up” (or out of the age group, i.e. U7 playing in U9, U9 playing in U11, etc.)  Conversely, no one may play down in a division for which they are not age eligible. Any questions should be directed to a board member.

U15:

Birthdates from September 1, 2001 to August 31, 2003 cannot turn 15 years old before September 1, 2015. 

WCLA will field one A team and as many B teams as necessary based upon enrollment and skill level. All teams will travel and compete within the Chester County Lacrosse Association (CCLA). Age eligible 9th graders may not play for both WCLA and their high school.U15 players typically have one or two practices and two games each week (usually on Thursday and Saturday). 2017 U15 Registration fee = $255

U13:

Birthdates from September 1, 2003 to August 31, 2005 cannot turn 13 years old before September 1, 2015.WCLA will field one A team and as many B and C teams as necessary based upon enrollment and skill level. All teams will travel and compete within the CCLA. The C division teams will have a greater emphasis on skill development. U13 players typically have one or two practices and two games each week (usually on Monday, Tuesday and Saturday).2017 U13 Registration fee = $255

U11:

Birthdates from September 1, 2005 to August 31, 2007 - cannot turn 11 years old before September 1, 2015.  WCLA will field two A teams and as many B and C teams as necessary based upon enrollment and skill level.   The teams will travel and compete within the CCLA.  
U11A players will have one or two practices per week and as many as two games each week (usually on Wednesday and Saturday). U11A Travel players are expected to make all practices and games.

Players registering but not chosen for a U11A team will be drafted onto a U11B or C team. U11 B teams will be divided into balanced teams based on enrollment and ability.  U11 B teams will practice one or two times per week, with one or two games per week to be usually played on Wednesday, Saturday or Sunday. Additional play dates may be added in addition to the regular schedule. 2017 U11 Registration fee = $255

U9:

Birthdates September 1, 2007 to August 31, 2009, cannot turn 9 years old before September 1, 2015.  Teams will be balanced based on enrollment. All teams will play primarily an “in-house” schedule combined with a few “Play Days” with other local youth lacrosse organizations.  The WCLA U9 program places an emphasis on introducing the game of lacrosse while building strong fundamentals and having fun.  Sessions will be conducted on Wednesday evenings and Saturday mornings.  Skill sessions will be conducted on Wednesday evenings with “in-house” scrimmage games on Saturday mornings.2017 U9 Registration Fee = $180

U7:

Birthdates September 1, 2009 to August 31, 2011, cannot turn 7 years old before September 1, 2015. Teams will be balanced based on enrollment. All teams will play primarily an “in-house” schedule combined with a few “Play Days” with other local youth lacrosse organizations.  The WCLA U7 schedule is now the same as U9, placing an emphasis on introducing the game of lacrosse while building strong fundamentals and having fun.  Sessions will be held on Saturday mornings only. 

PLEASE NOTE THAT U7 IS NOW A FULL EQUIPMENT DIVISION.

PLAYERS WILL BE REQUIRED TO PROVIDE FULL SET OF EQUIPMENT.

2017 U7 Registration fee = $140

  • 1 - Boys Lacrosse Stick
